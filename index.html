Born a Crime by Trebor Noah

"I wan born a crime...."
This line in the book is more than a title, it's the truth that shaped Trevor Noah's life. In this interesting and humories memoir, the book takes us through his South African childhood under the rule of apartheid with heart, humor, and love for his strong mother.
